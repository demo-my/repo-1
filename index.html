About

Our Values are as follows

Creative Discipline

We encourage our clients and employees to recognise creativity as a resource that should be nurtured for social and economic development.It should be managed delicately with personal attention to all the details that help produce great works of communication.

Professionalism

We take our work seriously.From receiving the client’s brief to delivering the final product, we aim to interact with everyone in the most professional manner. Timelines are sacrosanct and our services and products will most often exceed your expectations.

Innovation

We aim to ignite,nurture and apply our technical proficiency and analytical thinking to engage the target audiences’visual imagination in the most memorably way.In a world cluttered with multiple media messages.

Holistic Development

The creative process is both dynamic and adaptable,It considers all factors that are engaged in the expression of a message,As we build relations within our team and our clients,we strive to reflect the environment of growth and development.

Our Mission

To provide high standard and effective communication by utilising diverse media vehicles that ensure the best quality results are attained.

Our Vision

In Gecko Media Interactive our vision that makes us successful and going is to be the regional leader in creative expression using multiple media.

How we do it all

The Gecko Media Interactive Tree.

  • 1.

    Client Brief

    This process is the first attempt to describe the possible requirements of the project.The project brief is the key document upon which the design will be based.

  • 2.

    Market Research

    In this step the team takes the action or activity of gathering information about the clients' needs and preferences.

  • 3.

    Agency Brainstorm and Strategy

    This is the first step in the exploration phase of a new project,the team opens up to all ideas and possibilities,team members filters out the good ideas from the not-so-good ones inorder to come up in an agreement.

  • 4.

    Client Service make a Creative Brief

    In this part the client explains the ins and outs of a project for the creative team, agency, or designer who’ll be working on it.This step helps in shaping the overall strategy and goals for the project.

  • 5.

    Creative Team Design proposals

    In step the creative team sit down and come up with a design proposal that they will present to the client matching there requirements that they needed on there project.

  • 6.

    Presentation of Creative Drafts and Costing to Client

    After the creation of the proposal the creative team now presents the drafts and costings to the client so as to be aware of the pricing.

  • 7.

    Final Creative

    In this step the creative team come up with the final break down after taking to the client and knowing what they want.

  • 8.

    Production

    In this process the creative team collects all the information they gathered during there research and comes up with the final product of the project.

  • 9.

    Implementation

    After gathering everything that is needed in production, magic now happens in this step until the creative team comes up with the final delivery.

  • 10.

    Assessments

    After the final delivery know the estimation of the project happens in this step, when everything is fine its now handed over to the client.
